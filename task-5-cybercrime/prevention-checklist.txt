Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Never share your OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone
- Always check the website URL before logging in
- Don’t click on random or suspicious links
- Use strong and different passwords for accounts
- Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for payments or banking
- Double-check sender emails before trusting them
- Keep your phone and apps updated

If something goes wrong, report it at: cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
